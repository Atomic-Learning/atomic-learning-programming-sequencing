There are some special considerations when sequencing programming pages in the Atomic Learning platform. These tend to derive from the fact that there are many different programming languages and that programming tools can be applied across many different disciplines.

Language-Agnostic Pages

Many topics in programming will be applicable across many different programming languages. To avoid repetition for learners, pages covering these broad concepts should be introduced in language-agnostic pages. These pages aim to provide an understanding of the fundamental principles of programming that transcend specific languages.

In many cases, further pages will be needed to provide language-specific details and applications, and these would use the language-agnostic page as a prerequisite. However, these pages would not require reintroduction of the underlying concepts, behaviours and applications and would focus solely on language-specific syntax and behaviours.
